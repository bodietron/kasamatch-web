Privacy Policy

Last updated: 8 July 2026 · Applies to the KasaMatch mobile app (com.greenroomdigital.kasamatch), currently in closed beta.

KasaMatch ("KasaMatch", "we", "us") is a household-services marketplace operated by Greenroom Digital that connects homeowners with verified household helpers in the Philippines. This policy explains what personal data we collect, why, how we handle it, and your rights. Trust is the whole point of KasaMatch, and that starts with being clear about your data.

1. Who we are

Data controller: Greenroom Digital (KasaMatch). Contact: benlopcon@gmail.com. We are based in the Philippines and this service is offered to users in the Philippines.

2. Information we collect

We only collect what the service needs. We do not use your device camera, GPS, contacts, or the advertising ID.

3. How we use your information

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the KasaMatch service.
  • Verify helper identities and credentials, and prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Match homeowners and helpers, and enable messaging and bookings between them.
  • Send you service notifications (e.g., a new message or booking update).
  • Provide support, and keep the app secure, stable, and lawful.

4. How your information is shared

We do not sell your personal data. We share it only as follows:

  • With other users, as part of the service you choose: your public profile is visible to users you can be matched with; your name and messages are visible to people you chat with; and your saved home location is shared with a helper only after you confirm a booking with them. Government ID and selfie images are not shown to other users — they are restricted to you and our verification reviewers.
  • With our service providers (processors): we use Google Firebase (Authentication, Firestore, Cloud Storage, Cloud Functions, Cloud Messaging, Crashlytics, and Analytics) to run the app on our behalf. Google processes this data under Google's terms and security controls; see Google's privacy policy.
  • For legal reasons: if required by law, regulation, or a valid legal request, or to protect the rights, safety, and security of our users and the public.

5. Data security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS). Access to your data is controlled by server-side security rules: sensitive verification documents live in a private area readable only by you and authorized verification reviewers; your public profile and other data are scoped so users can only read what the service intends. No system is perfectly secure, but we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

6. Data retention

We keep your personal data for as long as your account is active and as needed to provide the service. If you delete your account, we delete or de-identify your personal data, except where we must retain limited information to comply with legal obligations or resolve disputes.

7. Your rights & choices

You may access, correct, or update most of your information in the app. You can also request that we delete your account and personal data. To make a request — or to exercise any right below — email benlopcon@gmail.com and we will respond within a reasonable time.

Philippines Data Privacy Act (RA 10173)

If you are in the Philippines, you have the rights of a data subject under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 — including the rights to be informed, to access, to object, to rectify, to erasure or blocking, and to data portability, and to lodge a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (privacy.gov.ph). We process your data based on your consent and on the necessity of providing the service you request.

8. Children

KasaMatch is intended for users aged 18 and over. It is not directed to children, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 18. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete it.

9. International processing

Our provider Google may process and store data on servers outside the Philippines. Where this happens, it is done under Google's security and data-protection commitments.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the app evolves (it is in active beta). We will change the "Last updated" date above and, for significant changes, notify you in the app. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact us

Questions or requests about this policy or your data: benlopcon@gmail.com.
