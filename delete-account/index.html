Delete your KasaMatch account

This page explains how to delete your KasaMatch account and personal data, and what happens to your information when you do. It applies to the KasaMatch app (com.greenroomdigital.kasamatch).

Option 1 — Delete in the app Fastest

  1. Open the KasaMatch app and sign in.
  2. Go to Profile (bottom-right).
  3. Tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm. Your account and data are deleted right away.

Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can no longer sign in, email us and we will delete your account for you:

  • Email benlopcon@gmail.com
  • Use the subject line "Delete my KasaMatch account"
  • Send it from the email address on your KasaMatch account, or tell us the phone number / name on the account so we can verify it's you.

We aim to process email requests within a few business days.

What gets deleted

  • Your profile (name, photo, bio, skills, availability).
  • Your verification images (government ID and selfie) — removed from storage within 72 hours.
  • Your chats, bookings, and reviews you authored are removed or de-identified.
  • Your login/authentication account.

What may be kept, and why

For safety and legal reasons we keep a small, minimal record that an account was verified (a "verification attestation" — with no ID image, ID number, or date of birth) for a limited period, and we retain records required to respond to an active safety report or a lawful request from authorities. These are deleted once no longer needed.

No payment data

KasaMatch does not process payments and does not store any card, bank, or wallet information, so there is no payment data to delete.

Questions about your data? Email benlopcon@gmail.com. See also our Privacy Policy.
